Allamew Analytics

The objective of Allamew is to provide businesses with information on customer flows, such as no of walkbys and store entries, and how long customers stay for.

Privacy

No data is collected that can identify persons; we collect only a phone or laptop's MAC address, wifi signal strength, and the timestamp of the signal. To understand more, go here.

Installation

Installation is currently customised as per the store. However, an internet connection and a powerpoint are needed. The actual sensor is about 5cm squared, so can fit conveniently almost anywhere.
