Enter your MAC address in the box below, and Allamew will stop collection of, and delete, all data pertaining to that address

How to Find Your MAC Address

Phone Type To find address, go to:
iPhone Settings > General > About > WiFi Address
Android (some versions) Settings > About > Status > WiFi MAC Address
Android (other versions) Settings > About > Hardware information > WiFi MAC Address
Windows Phone Settings > About > More info > WiFi Address
Blackberry (some versions) Options > Status > WLAN MAC
Blackberry (other versions) Setup > Options > Device > Device and Status Information > WLAN MAC
